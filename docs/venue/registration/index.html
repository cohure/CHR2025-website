Registration

We are using EasyChair to handle conference registration. After logging in, select the link Registration ↣ start registration. If you are an author of a paper, please use the same login that you had for your submission. Otherwise, you can use an existing account or create a new EasyChair account for free.

Registrations will remain open until 11:59 PM on November 20, 2025, in the last time zone globally.

Register Here

→

The registration fees for the conference are as follows:

  • Regular - high & upper-middle income countries (€100)
  • Regular - low & lower-middle income countries (€70)
  • Regular - online (€30)
  • Student - high & upper-middle income countries (€50)
  • Student - low & lower-middle income countries (€35)
  • Student - online (free)

There is an optional conference dinner (11 December) that can be added during registration for €90.
