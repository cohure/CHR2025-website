Venue

The CHR2025 conference will take place from the 9th to the 12th of December at the Luxembourg Centre for Contemporary and Digital History (C²DH), University of Luxembourg. The conference will be held at the Belval Campus, a historic former steel industry site that has been transformed into a modern educational and economic hub.
