Facilities on Belval Campus

Belval Campus is the main hub of the University of Luxembourg, offering a modern and vibrant academic environment that integrates education, research, and innovation. It is located on the site of a former steelworks in Esch-sur-Alzette, symbolizing the country’s commitment to knowledge and technology.

Key features and facilities

  • Various restaurants and bars offering Korean, Asian, Luxembourgish, Italian, and other cuisines.
  • Belval Plaza is a mall with restaurants, shops, a drugstore, and a cinema.
  • Luxembourg Learning Centre (LLC) is the library campus, a must-visit.
  • Blast Furnaces where a visit is organized as part of the social events.
  • Medical House (Maisons médicales de garde) provides emergency care without an appointment on weekdays between 8 pm and midnight. For hospital emergencies, please call 112, the European Emergency Number.
  • Pharmacy where you can obtain basic medications like paracetamol and cough/throat syrup, while other medications may require a doctor’s prescription.
