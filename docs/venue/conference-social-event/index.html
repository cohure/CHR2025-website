Conference Social Event

Join us for the conference dinner on the 11th of December at Come à la Maison.

We look forward to sharing this special dining experience with you. Be sure to register for the CHR 2025 conference and conference social event.

Conference Dinner Transport

We can take you to the conference dinner by private bus that will leave from the Ibis Hotel in Belval at 7:15 PM. The return bus will depart from the restaurant at 10:30 PM back to Belval. If you wish to use this service, you need to register with us as places are limited. Please fill this questionnaire which will remain open only as long as there are still available places.

Otherwise, feel free to use public transport in Luxembourg which is free.
